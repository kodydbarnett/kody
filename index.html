Professional Summary

About Kody

I’m a Senior HR Business Partner (HRBP) and People Operations leader with 8 years of progressive experience in employee relations, workforce planning, compliance, and HR infrastructure across manufacturing, PE-backed, and multi-site environments — from 50 to 1,200 employees.

I’ve served as the primary HR partner to CEOs, division presidents, and C-suite leaders, independently owning the full spectrum of HRBP work without relying on a deep support structure. My environments have ranged from PE-backed growth organizations and government contract research to multi-site retail, healthcare, and precision manufacturing — each requiring different compliance depth, stakeholder approaches, and workforce strategies.

What I bring that most HR leaders can’t: I’ve done this work where there’s no room for theory. I’ve resolved active EEOC claims, conducted C-suite and executive-level investigations, led M&A due diligence across 13 acquired locations, corrected FLSA misclassifications before they became lawsuits, and built compensation frameworks from scratch — all while staying the trusted advisor leaders actually call when the stakes are high.
